# Support

## Contact

- General support: support@dryft.site
- Privacy requests: privacy@dryft.site
- Legal questions: legal@dryft.site

## What to Include

Providing the details below helps the team resolve issues faster:

- App and platform (mobile, web, desktop, VR)
- App version or build number
- Device and OS version
- Steps to reproduce
- Screenshots or screen recordings (if applicable)
- Approximate time the issue occurred

## Bug Reports (Internal)

If you have repository access, open a bug in the project tracker and include:

- Logs or error messages
- A minimal reproduction path
- Whether the issue is consistent or intermittent

## Safety and Abuse

Use in-app block and report tools for immediate safety actions. For urgent safety concerns, include "URGENT" in the subject line of your email.

## Security Issues

Please follow the guidance in `SECURITY.md` for vulnerability disclosure.
