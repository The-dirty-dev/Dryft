# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: January 2026**

Dryft ("we", "our", or "us") operates the Dryft mobile application and VR application (collectively, the "Service"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our Service.

## 1. Age Requirement

**Dryft is strictly for users 18 years of age or older.** We do not knowingly collect information from anyone under 18. By using our Service, you represent that you are at least 18 years old.

We use third-party age verification services to verify user ages. If we discover that a user is under 18, we will immediately terminate their account and delete their data.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Information You Provide

- **Account Information**: Email address, display name, profile photo, and bio
- **Verification Data**: Age verification documents (processed by third-party services, not stored by us)
- **Payment Information**: Processed securely through Stripe; we do not store full payment details
- **Profile Preferences**: Matching preferences, interests, and settings
- **Communications**: Messages, chat history with matches, and support communications
- **Session Participation**: Companion session codes, display names, and participation data you submit

### 2.2 Information Collected Automatically

- **Device Information**: Device type, operating system, unique device identifiers
- **Usage Data**: Features used, time spent, interaction patterns
- **VR Telemetry**: Avatar positions, interactions (anonymized for analytics)
- **Log Data**: IP address, access times, app crashes, and errors
- **Push Tokens**: Device tokens for notifications (FCM/APNs)
- **Haptic Device Metadata**: Device name, capabilities, and connection status
- **Call Metadata**: Call initiation, duration, and signaling events
- **Session Metadata**: Session creation, join/leave timestamps, and participant counts
- **Analytics Events**: Client event names and timestamps used for product analytics

### 2.3 Information from Third Parties

- **Age Verification Providers**: Verification status only (documents are not shared with us)
- **Payment Processors**: Transaction status and billing address

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

- Provide, maintain, and improve the Service
- Process transactions and send related information
- Verify your age and identity
- Match you with other users based on preferences
- Send notifications about matches, messages, and Service updates
- Monitor and analyze usage patterns and trends
- Detect, prevent, and address technical issues and abuse
- Enforce our Terms of Service and protect user safety

## 4. Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information in the following circumstances:

### 4.1 With Other Users
- Your display name, profile photo, and bio are visible to other users
- Your approximate location (city-level) may be shown to potential matches
- Messages are shared with your matched users

### 4.2 With Service Providers
- **Stripe**: Payment processing
- **Age Verification Services**: Identity verification
- **Cloud Providers**: Data hosting and storage
- **Analytics Services**: Usage analytics (anonymized)
- **Push Providers**: Firebase/APNs for notification delivery

### 4.3 For Legal Reasons
- To comply with legal obligations
- To protect and defend our rights and property
- To prevent or investigate wrongdoing
- To protect user safety

## 5. Data Retention

- **Account Data**: Retained while your account is active, deleted within 30 days of account deletion
- **Messages**: Retained for 1 year after last activity, then deleted
- **Verification Data**: Verification status retained; documents are not stored
- **Device Tokens**: Retained while your account is active or until unregistered
- **Call Metadata**: Retained for operational and safety purposes, then deleted
- **Session Metadata**: Retained for up to 90 days
- **Usage Analytics**: Anonymized and retained for up to 2 years

## 6. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including:

- TLS encryption for data in transit
- AES-256 encryption for data at rest
- Regular security audits and penetration testing
- Access controls and employee training
- Secure data centers with physical security

## 7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

- **Access**: Request a copy of your personal data
- **Correction**: Request correction of inaccurate data
- **Deletion**: Request deletion of your data
- **Portability**: Receive your data in a portable format
- **Opt-out**: Opt out of certain data processing
- **Withdraw Consent**: Withdraw consent for data processing

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@dryft.site.

### 7.1 California Residents (CCPA)
California residents have additional rights under the CCPA, including the right to know what personal information is collected and the right to opt out of the sale of personal information. We do not sell personal information.

### 7.2 European Residents (GDPR)
European residents have rights under GDPR including access, rectification, erasure, restriction, portability, and objection. Our legal basis for processing is consent and legitimate interests.

## 8. International Data Transfers

Your data may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place, including Standard Contractual Clauses where required.

## 9. Children's Privacy

Our Service is not intended for anyone under 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

## 10. Third-Party Links

Our Service may contain links to third-party websites or services. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third parties.

## 11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by:
- Posting the new policy on our website
- Sending an email notification
- Displaying an in-app notice

Your continued use of the Service after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
