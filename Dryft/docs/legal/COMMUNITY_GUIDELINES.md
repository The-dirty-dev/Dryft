# Community Guidelines

**Last Updated: January 2026**

Welcome to Dryft. These guidelines help maintain a safe, respectful, and enjoyable environment for all members of our adult community.

## Our Core Values

1. **Respect** - Treat others as you want to be treated
2. **Consent** - Always ask and respect boundaries
3. **Safety** - Look out for yourself and others
4. **Authenticity** - Be genuine and honest

---

## Age Requirement

**Dryft is exclusively for adults 18 years and older.**

- You must be 18+ to use Dryft
- Age verification is mandatory
- Discussing or depicting minors in any sexual context is strictly prohibited and will result in immediate permanent ban and report to authorities

---

## Respectful Interactions

### Do
- Be kind and courteous
- Respect others' time and boundaries
- Accept "no" gracefully
- Give genuine compliments
- Have meaningful conversations

### Don't
- Harass, bully, or intimidate
- Send unsolicited explicit messages
- Continue contact after being asked to stop
- Make discriminatory remarks
- Pressure anyone into anything

---

## Consent is Mandatory

### In Chat
- Ask before sending explicit content
- Respect when someone declines
- Don't share private conversations

### In VR
- Ask before initiating intimate interactions
- Respect personal space
- Stop immediately if asked
- Use safe words/signals
- The panic button is always available

### For Haptic Devices
- Get explicit consent before sending haptic signals
- Stop when asked
- Respect intensity preferences

---

## Profile Guidelines

### Photos
**Allowed:**
- Clear photos of yourself
- Tasteful/artistic photos
- Photos showing your interests

**Not Allowed:**
- Photos of other people without consent
- Explicit photos in main profile (save for private sharing)
- Fake or misleading photos
- Photos of minors
- Illegal content

### Bio
**Allowed:**
- Honest description of yourself
- Your interests and what you're looking for
- Humor and personality

**Not Allowed:**
- Hate speech or slurs
- Contact info (for safety)
- Illegal solicitation
- Spam or advertisements

---

## Content Rules

### Explicitly Prohibited Content
- Child sexual abuse material (CSAM) - **Zero tolerance, immediate ban, law enforcement report**
- Non-consensual intimate images
- Bestiality or animal abuse
- Extreme violence or gore
- Terrorism or violent extremism
- Human trafficking
- Doxxing or personal information sharing

### Restricted Content (Private Sharing Only)
- Explicit sexual content (between consenting matched adults)
- Nudity

### Commercial Content
- No spam or advertising
- No solicitation of services
- No promotion of external platforms
- Creator accounts must follow separate guidelines

---

## VR-Specific Guidelines

### Public Spaces (Lounge, etc.)
- Keep interactions appropriate for public viewing
- No explicit activities
- Respect others' experiences
- Keep voice chat civil

### Private Booths
- Explicit interactions allowed between consenting adults
- All consent rules still apply
- No recording without explicit consent
- Exit immediately if uncomfortable

### Avatar Behavior
- Don't invade others' virtual space uninvited
- Respond to rejection respectfully
- Don't trap or corner other users
- Use appropriate gestures

---

## Safety Features

### Panic Button
- Available everywhere in VR
- Immediately exits any situation
- No consequences for using it
- Reports are optional

### Blocking
- Block anyone for any reason
- Blocked users cannot contact you
- Blocks are mutual and permanent
- No notification to blocked user

### Reporting
- Report violations to our moderation team
- Include details and evidence if possible
- Reports are confidential
- False reports may result in penalties

---

## Moderation & Enforcement

### How We Moderate
- AI-assisted content detection
- User reports review
- Random audits
- Trust and safety team

### Enforcement Actions

| Violation Level | Examples | Action |
|-----------------|----------|--------|
| Minor | First-time rudeness, mild spam | Warning |
| Moderate | Harassment, inappropriate public behavior | Temporary suspension (1-7 days) |
| Severe | Repeated violations, targeted harassment | Extended suspension (30 days) |
| Critical | CSAM, non-consensual sharing, violence | Permanent ban + law enforcement |

### Appeals
- Email: appeals@dryft.site
- Include your account info and explanation
- Response within 5 business days
- Decisions are final for critical violations

---

## Protecting Yourself

### Online Safety
- Don't share personal info (address, workplace, full name)
- Use the in-app messaging
- Trust your instincts
- Report suspicious behavior

### Meeting IRL
- Meet in public places first
- Tell someone where you're going
- Have your own transportation
- Trust your gut - leave if uncomfortable

### Financial Safety
- Never send money to matches
- Report financial requests immediately
- We never ask for payment outside the app

---

## Reporting Crimes

If you experience or witness:
- Threats of violence
- Sexual assault
- Human trafficking
- Child exploitation

**Contact law enforcement immediately.**

We cooperate fully with legal investigations and provide evidence as required by law.

**Emergency**: Call 911 (US) or local emergency services
**NCMEC CyberTipline**: CyberTipline.org
**National Sexual Assault Hotline**: 1-800-656-4673
